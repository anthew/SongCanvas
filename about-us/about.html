Background

Currently, there are many general purpose video editors and also some that gear towards music videos specifically. Many of these users have vast video editing skills and already have gained experience using these platforms. On the contrary, users encounter challenges with attempting to adapt into an application that they are unaware of. In addition, users struggle with translating their vision into the video editor application because they don't have the skillset for that application yet. Moreover, video editing softwares varies in cost and features compared to others, complicating the decision-making process for ideal tools to use.

Objectives

SongCanvas aspires to deliver a transformative and cutting-edge user experience tailored specifically for the needs of music video creators or event hosts. The core mission is to go beyond the competition and actively offer an entirely new platform of user-friendliness, setting a precedent in the industry. This approach entails a comprehensive revamp of the user experience that aims to simplify and streamline the music video production process from initial to final.
